## Requirements

To reproduce the data preparation and analysis steps:

- **Python 3.9+**
- **Libraries:**
  - pandas==2.2.2
  - numpy==1.26.4
  - matplotlib==3.8.4
  - seaborn==0.13.2
  - jupyter==1.0.0 (optional, for exploration)
  
For data visualisation:

- **Tableau Public** (version 2024.2 or later)
  - Dashboard: *YouTube Trending Dashboard*
  - Data source: `trending_by_time.csv`
